Nachos

Big cheesy nachos

In this easy nacho recipe, we'll show you how to create a delicious game day snack. It is as easy a few simple steps and will leave your guests satsfied!

Here are your needed ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • Cheese of your choosing
  • Pickled jalapenos
  • Mexican style chorizo

On to the steps

  1. Sautee chorizo in a small saucepan for 6 to 8 minutes until cooked through
  2. While chorizo cooks, place tortilla chips on to a plate
  3. Dress the chips with cheese and microwave for about 50 seconds or until cheese is fully melted
  4. Remove the nachos from the microwave and top with the cooked chorizo and your pickled jalapenos
  5. Enjoy!

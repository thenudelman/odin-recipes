Taco Supreme

These tacos are a versatile dish, perfect for anything from birthdays, sporting events, and even snacking.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • Ground beef
  • Cilantro
  • Monterrey Jack cheese
  • Salsa
  • Taco seasoning packet
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Recipe Steps

  1. Cook your ground beef until brown, mix in packet of taco seasoning
  2. While beef is cooking, chop up lettuce and tomato into slices
  3. Place your taco shells on a plate and create a base layer of cheese
  4. When beef is browned, spoon it into the taco shells on top of your cheese
  5. Layer on lettuce, tomato, and some more cheese
  6. Garnish with chopped cilantro and salsa
  7. Enjoy
